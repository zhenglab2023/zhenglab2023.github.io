YUXUAN ZHENG, PH.D.

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR

Yuxuan Zheng is the principal investigator at Human Phenome Institute in Fudan University. He started his lab in 2023 to investigate the multi-dimensional regulatory mechanisms of development and disease with the single-cell genomics technology to better understand the origin, development, and progression of diseases. He completed postdoctoral research at Institute of Microbiology at Chinese Academy of Sciences with Professor George Fu Gao. He received his Ph.D. in Bioinformatics & Cell Biology from Peking University with Professor Fuchou Tang.
